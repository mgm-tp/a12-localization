## Support Channels

### A12 Community Edition
- **General information** is available at https://geta12.com/#/support/support-overview
- A dedicated **A12 Community Forum** is available for community users, accessible via https://discourse.geta12.com/. Register to post a topic.
- Before posting, please review the **technical documentation** at https://GetA12.com, where the latest A12 installers are also available.
- **mgm does not commit response times** for community users.  


### A12 Enterprise Edition
- Public information and discussions can be joined in the A12 Community Forum via https://discourse.geta12.com/. The content is public, please do not post sensitive information.
- Enterprise customers may open **support tickets** through the ticket system at https://a12support.mgm-tp.com. Access requires **contractually designated accounts**.  
- To ensure efficient processing, please include all necessary information for analysis in your ticket.
- Response times are defined by the **Service Level Agreement (SLA)** of your Enterprise Subscription and Support agreement.  

---

## Scope of Support

- Support is provided **only for the latest A12 release** and designated **LTS releases** - check [A12 Release Lines](https://geta12.com/#/releases/release-lines)
- A valid support request must include:
  - A **clear and detailed description** of the issue  
  - The **exact A12 release** in use  
  - The **error message** received  
  - **Relevant log files**, if applicable  
- It is recommended to indicate the **impact on production**:
  - Complete production outage (Severity 1)
  - Major disruption (Severity 2)
  - Limited restriction (Severity 3)
  - Minor issue (Severity 4)
